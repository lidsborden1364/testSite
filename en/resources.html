Div Surg App

Since 2015, our app has been trusted by thousands of CAF Personnel by allowing them to tap into thousands of pieces of evidence-based research for making better-informed health care decisions. Our mobile-responsive design allows the Div Surg App to respond to the user's behaviour and environment based on screen size and orientation of any device, whether they are accessing it on a DWAN desktop or laptop computer, tablet or smartphone.



R2MR App

What is Road to Mental Readiness (R2MR) Mobile Application? • It is a mobile training tool (adjunct to classroom training) developed to improve short-term performance and long-term mental health outcomes • Provides the tools and resources required to manage and support CAF member, family members and the general public. R2MR training is layered and tailored to meet the relevant demands and responsibilities CAF personnel encounter at each stage of their career and while on deployment.
