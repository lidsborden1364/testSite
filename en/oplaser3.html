Op LASER 3 Training

The Canadian Forces are currently in the largest mobilization of Canadian military health personnel since the Second World War. CFHS personnel are busy preparing training, mounting, deploying and conducting operations.

You are now called by your country to work beside healthcare providers. We are taking the time to train you because it is worth it, because you are of great added value to the healthcare team in that non clinical support role. You will have to be careful not to become a perceived healthcare provider, as it is not what we are training you to do.

The most important for you to remember from this training is the primacy of

  1. The safety of the people you are going to help;
  2. The safety of those around you;
  3. Your own safety.

Also, you must ask questions…

  1. Ask questions if there something you don’t understand, especially in the practical components of your training modules such as the Personal Protective Equipment;
  2. Ask questions before doing something you have not been trained to do;  
  3. Ask questions if you are not sure...

Finally, prepare yourself mentally for the operating environment. Know how to reach for help either for yourself or for someone around you, it is your responsibility.

You are about to live a very special experience, and our CFHS team is very thankful of the support and the professionalism you will show working beside us.

Thank you.

Signed by

Commander N. Gauthier
Commandant
